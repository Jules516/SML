About Us


SML NFNIT ENTERPRISES is a leading distributor of specialized products, wholesale & retail, from apparels to corporate giveaways and almost everything our clients require. We have provided quality and exceptional services to our customers from private entities to government institutions. SML NFIT ENTERPRISES is a product of experience formed by individuals with broad experience in varies industries. We are engaged in the supply of wide range of products and services across many industries from Trading Supplies, Manufacturing Supplies, Construction Supplies, Automotive Supplies, Electronics, Medical Protective Equipment & Apparel to Home & Office Security Supplies.
We continue to expand our array of latest & innovative products and services to provide our customers. With a range of both standardized and customized products and services available, we aim not only to meet expectation but also ensure our customer satisfaction
